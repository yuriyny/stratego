Stratego Rules
1 All numbered pieces can only move 1 space in horizontal or vertical directions.
 The only exception to this is piece-2, which can move more than 1 space.
 However, piece-2 cannot leap over pieces.
 The bomb and flag pieces cannot be moved so place them with caution.
2 When numbered pieces engage, only the higher value will survive.
 The only exception to this is that piece-1 can defeat piece-10.
 Both pieces will be lost when values are equal.
 Only piece-3 can defeat bomb pieces. Any other piece will be defeated by the bomb.
 Any piece can defeat the flag piece.
 You cannot attack your own pieces.
3 Enemy pieces will be revealed/shown as you combat them.
4 The game is over when your or the enemies flag is defeated.
5 The notifications/messages are your friend.
